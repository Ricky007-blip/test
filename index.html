About Me

With over 15 years of experience in vocal performance and ceremony hosting, I bring a unique blend of artistry and compassion to every event. My journey began in classical music training, which evolved into a passion for serving families during life's most significant moments.

Whether celebrating love at weddings or honoring life at memorial services, I believe in the transformative power of music and carefully crafted words to heal, inspire, and connect.

My approach combines professional excellence with genuine empathy, ensuring each ceremony is as unique as the individuals it celebrates.
